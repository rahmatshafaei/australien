Australia's Tradition

Anzac day

Anzac Day is celebrated on April 25 and is probably Australia's most important national day. This day stands for Australian and New zealand army corps. Australian and New Zealand forces fought during the First World War.

Boxing day test

Many important sports events are held on Boxing Day. Because the Boxing Day test match is a cricket game at Melbourne which is the second biggest city in Australia on Cricket Ground. The game is between the Australian National cricket team and other national teams touring Australia. The match starts on Boxing Day and may last up to five days. Between 1999 and 2007, the Australian cricket team won all of the Boxing Day Test matches and there was a huge victory for Australiens national team.

Muck up day

A Senior prank , also known as muck-up day in Australia and the United kingdom. This is a type of organized prank pulled by students which is the senior class of a school, college or university. They did that because of chaos throughout the institution. But usually the prank was carried out at the end of the senior school year as a going away mark on the school.

Triple j hottest 100

Triple J Hottest 100 is an annual music listening voice hosted by Triple J, Australia's publicly funded national radio station. In an online poll held two weeks before the new year, members of the public are invited to vote for their favorite Australian and alternative music of the year.
