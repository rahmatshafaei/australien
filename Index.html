Many important sports events are held on Boxing Day. Because the Boxing Day test match is a cricket game at Melbourne which is the second biggest city in Australia on Cricket Ground. The game is between the Australian National cricket team and other national teams touring Australia. The match starts on Boxing Day and may last up to five days. Between 1999 and 2007, the Australian cricket team won all of the Boxing Day Test matches and there was a huge victory for Australiens national team.
